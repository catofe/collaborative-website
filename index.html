ABOUT THE CAFE

The Cafe was founded in Cagayan de Oro City by Mr. Dave Baran, the best businessman in Ohio Suites, with the power of friendship, he set out to find and construct the best cafe in the whole wide world. Together with his friends, they set out a journey to find the dragon bean for the cafe company to flourish.

In addition to our full espresso and brew bar menu, we serve fresh made-to-order breakfast and lunch sandwiches, as well as a selection of sides and salads and other good stuff such as the best dances to break it down. You can see the owner live doing the Nae nae!

"Use products from nature for what it's worth - but never too early, nor too late." Fresh is the new sweet and offense.

Chef, Coffeeist and Owner: Anastasio

Opening hours: everyday from 6am to 5pm.
